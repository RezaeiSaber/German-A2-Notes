In German, adjectives have three forms: **Positiv** (base), **Komparativ** (comparative - "er"), and **Superlativ** (superlative - "est").

### Irregular Forms (The Big Three)
These adjectives are irregular and must be memorized as they do not follow standard patterns.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

## üéÅ Verbs with Two Objects (Dative + Accusative) [32:51](https://youtube.com/watch?v=xAkmLFk3B5U&t=1971s)
