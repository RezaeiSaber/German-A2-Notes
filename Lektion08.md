## 1. Comparison of Adjectives: Komparativ & Superlativ [02:36](https://youtube.com/watch?v=3DX79ZqCXfw&t=156s)

Adjectives have three levels: **Positiv** (base form), **Komparativ** (comparative - "more"), and **Superlativ** (superlative - "most").

### Irregular Exceptions (Must Memorize) [03:01](https://youtube.com/watch?v=3DX79ZqCXfw&t=181s)

| Positiv | Komparativ | Superlativ |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **gut** (good) | **besser** (better) | **am besten** (best) |
| **viel** (much/many) | **mehr** (more) | **am meisten** (most) |
| **gern** (gladly) | **lieber** (prefer/rather) | **am liebsten** (favorite/most of all) |

### Regular Patterns and Spelling Changes [04:01](https://youtube.com/watch?v=3DX79ZqCXfw&t=241s)

* **Rule:** Add `-er` for Komparativ. Add `am` + `-sten` for Superlativ.
* **Spelling Tip:** If an adjective ends in `-t`, `-s`, or `-z`, add an `-e-` before `-sten` for easier pronunciation (e.g., *am k√§ltesten*).
